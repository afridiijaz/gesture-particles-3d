About ParticleMancer

ParticleMancer is an interactive 3D particle experience built with Three.js and MediaPipe Hands. It uses your webcam to detect hand gestures in real time and translates them into stunning visual effects.

  • 🎨 8 particle shapes — Heart, Flower, Saturn, Firework, Spiral, Star, DNA, Galaxy
  • 🌈 7 colour themes — Rainbow, Fire, Ocean, Neon, Gold, Aurora, Sakura
  • 🖐 5 gesture controls — Expand, Compress, Attract, Resize, Cycle shapes
  • 🎮 Orbit controls — Click & drag to rotate the scene
  • ⌨️ Keyboard shortcuts — Keys 1–8 switch shapes, Space randomises colours

Built with pure vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no build tools.

🤚 Gesture Controls

  • ✊ Fist — Compress particles
  • 🖐 Open Hand — Expand particles
  • ✌️ Peace / Two — Next shape
  • 👆 Point — Attract to finger
  • 🤏 Pinch — Change size
